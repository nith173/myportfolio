Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer a wide range of graphic design services including brand identity design, digital marketing materials, product photography, social media graphics, and print design.

How long does a project typically take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Simple designs can take 1-3 days, while comprehensive brand projects may take 1-2 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide! I'm experienced in remote collaboration and can accommodate different time zones to ensure smooth communication throughout the project.

What file formats do you deliver?

I deliver all projects in multiple formats including high-resolution JPG, PNG, PDF, and editable source files (AI, PSD) so you have everything you need for various applications.
